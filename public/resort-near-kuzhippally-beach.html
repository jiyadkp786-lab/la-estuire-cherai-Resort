About Kuzhippally Beach

Kuzhippally Beach is a serene, relatively uncrowded beach on Vypin Island, located just south of Cherai Beach. It's known for its clear waters, peaceful environment, and beautiful sunsets over the Arabian Sea. Unlike the more popular Cherai Beach, Kuzhippally offers a quieter, more private beach experience.

La Estuaire Cherai is one of the closest resorts to Kuzhippally Beach - approximately 5 minutes by vehicle from our resort entrance. This makes us the perfect base for visitors who want to explore both Kuzhippally Beach and Cherai Beach during their Vypin Island stay.

FAQ - Resort Near Kuzhippally Beach

How far is La Estuaire Cherai from Kuzhippally Beach?
La Estuaire Cherai is approximately 5 minutes by vehicle from Kuzhippally Beach. Our resort is located in the Cherai area of Vypin Island - from where Kuzhippally Beach is a short and easy drive. We are also just 500 metres from Cherai Beach itself.
What is Kuzhippally Beach like?
Kuzhippally Beach is a serene and relatively uncrowded beach on Vypin Island, Kochi. It's known for its clean sands, clear waters, peaceful environment, and beautiful sunsets. It's a great alternative or complement to the more popular Cherai Beach - perfect for those seeking a quieter beach experience near Kochi.
Can I access multiple beaches from La Estuaire Cherai?
Yes! La Estuaire Cherai's central location on Vypin Island gives you convenient access to multiple beaches: Cherai Beach is 500 metres away (walking distance), Kuzhippally Beach is 5 minutes by vehicle, and Munambam Beach near Munambam Harbour is about 15 minutes away.
Is Kuzhippally Beach good for swimming?
Kuzhippally Beach generally has calm to moderate waves and is suitable for swimming. However, as with all Kerala beaches, sea conditions vary seasonally. The ocean is calmest from October to March. During monsoon months (June"September), waves can be stronger - always check conditions before swimming.
