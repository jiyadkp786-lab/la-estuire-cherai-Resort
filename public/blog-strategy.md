## Blog Content Guidelines

**Ideal Word Count:** 1,000–2,000 words per post
**Structure:** H1 → H2 sections → H3 subsections → FAQ at the end
**Images:** Include ALT text with keywords (e.g., "La Estuaire Cherai — resort near Cherai Beach, Vypin Island")
**CTA:** Every post must end with a CTA linking to homepage or relevant landing page
**Internal Links:** Minimum 2–3 internal links per post
**Meta Description:** 150–155 characters, include primary keyword naturally
