Cherai Beach

Everything You Need to Know About Cherai Beach

Cherai Beach is one of the most scenic and pristine beaches in Kerala, located on the northern tip of Vypin Island, Kochi. What makes Cherai Beach truly special is its unique geography " the beach runs alongside serene backwaters, so you can swim in the Arabian Sea and watch backwater canals within the same view.

The beach is known for its golden sands, calm waves (compared to other Kerala beaches), occasional dolphin sightings, and the presence of Chinese fishing nets. It's ideal for swimming, morning walks, sunset watching, and photography.

La Estuaire Cherai is just 500 metres from this beautiful beach " the closest boutique resort you can find in the area.
