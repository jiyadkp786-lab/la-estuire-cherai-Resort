The Best of Both Worlds: Cherai Beach & Backwaters

Cherai is famous worldwide for its narrow land strip, bounded by the Arabian Sea on the west and quiet backwater canals on the east. Most resorts only offer one or the other. La Estuaire Cherai is strategically positioned to offer you both: we are located right next to the peaceful lake backwaters, yet just a short 5-minute walk (500 metres) from the main beach front.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions - Beach & Backwater Resort

Is La Estuaire located directly on the beach?
We are located just 500 metres from Cherai Beach (a short 5-minute walk). This allows our guests to enjoy the beach easily while staying in a quieter, more peaceful backwater setting.
Do you offer backwater tours or kayaking?
Yes! We offer kayaking directly in the backwaters adjoining our property. It is one of the most popular activities for guests staying at La Estuaire.
Can we see the sunset from the resort?
You can watch spectacular sunsets by walking 5 minutes to Cherai Beach. The backwater side of our property offers beautiful sunrise views.
