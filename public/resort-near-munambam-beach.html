Explore Munambam Beach & Harbour from La Estuaire

Munambam Beach (also known as Muziris Golden Beach) is located at the northern end of Vypin Island, Ernakulam. Known as the point where the Periyar River flows into the Arabian Sea, it offers spectacular sunset views and a serene, less crowded beach experience. La Estuaire Cherai is positioned just a 15-minute drive from Munambam Beach, making it the perfect gateway resort to explore northern Vypin.

FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions - Resort near Munambam

How far is La Estuaire Cherai from Munambam Beach?
La Estuaire Cherai is approximately 15 minutes away by vehicle. The route is a direct drive north along the scenic Cherai Beach Road and Munambam Road.
Can we see dolphins at Munambam Beach?
Yes. Munambam Harbour and the meeting point of the river and sea are popular spots for dolphin sightings, especially in the early mornings.
Is Munambam Beach safe for swimming?
Yes, the beach features a clean, shallow sandy coastline. It is generally safer and less crowded compared to other commercial beaches in Kochi.
