FAQ

FAQ - Weekend Getaway Near Kochi

How far is La Estuaire Cherai from Kochi?
La Estuaire Cherai is approximately 30-45 minutes from Ernakulam (Kochi city center) by road, depending on traffic. You can reach us via the Vypin Island bridge/road or using the Vypeen ferry from Ernakulam. It's one of the closest beach resort getaways from Kochi.
Is Cherai a good weekend getaway from Kochi?
Absolutely. Cherai is widely considered one of the best weekend getaway destinations near Kochi. The combination of a beautiful beach, serene backwaters, peaceful Vypin Island environment, and authentic Kerala experiences - all just 30-45 minutes from the city - make it ideal for a 2-night or 3-night escape.
How many nights is ideal for a Cherai weekend getaway?
For a complete Cherai experience, we recommend at least 2 nights (Friday to Sunday). This gives you enough time to relax at the beach, enjoy the pool, go kayaking, explore Kuzhippally Beach, and savor Kerala cuisine at Joe's Cuisine. One night is possible but two nights is much more relaxing.
Is La Estuaire suitable for couples on a weekend getaway from Kochi?
Yes! La Estuaire Cherai is a popular couple-friendly resort for weekend getaways from Kochi. Our Lake View Premium Deluxe and Super Deluxe rooms, combined with backwater views, beach proximity, and a peaceful resort atmosphere, create the perfect romantic weekend escape near Kochi.
Is La Estuaire suitable for family weekend getaways from Kochi?
Absolutely. La Estuaire Cherai is family-friendly and an excellent choice for a family weekend getaway from Kochi. With spacious rooms, a swimming pool, Cherai Beach access, kayaking, authentic Kerala dining, and free parking - it's a complete family holiday experience near Kochi.
What is the best time for a weekend getaway to Cherai from Kochi?
Cherai is beautiful year-round. October to March is ideal for beach activities with calm seas and pleasant weather. June to September (monsoon season) is stunning for lush green landscapes and backwater experiences. April-May is hot but the resort pool makes it comfortable. All seasons have their own unique charm in Cherai.
